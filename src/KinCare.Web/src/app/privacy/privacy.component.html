Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

This Privacy Policy describes how KinCare ("KinCare," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our senior living transport coordination platform (the "Service"), used by facility coordinators, organization administrators, and transport vendors ("drivers").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information in the course of operating the Service:

  • Account information — name, email address, and role for coordinators and administrators who log in to KinCare.
  • Resident information — first and last name, and transport-related needs (e.g., wheelchair, oxygen, stretcher, walker requirements) and driver notes, entered by facility staff for the purpose of coordinating safe transport. This information is limited to what is reasonably necessary to arrange appropriate transportation.
  • Vendor/driver information — name, phone number, and optionally a photo, company name, and service area, provided by facility coordinators when onboarding a transport vendor.
  • Ride and location information — pickup and destination addresses, scheduled times, ride status history, and, only while a ride is actively in progress and only from the assigned vendor's device, GPS coordinates used to power live trip tracking.
  • Communications — SMS messages sent to and received from vendor phone numbers in connection with ride dispatch and status updates.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate the Service: booking rides, dispatching vendors, tracking ride status, and providing live location updates to the coordinating facility.
  • To send SMS notifications to transport vendors for ride dispatch, status check-ins, and related updates, and push notifications to coordinators for ride and escalation alerts.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and tenant isolation of the Service across the organizations we serve.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. SMS Messaging

Transport vendors are enrolled to receive SMS messages related to ride dispatch and status updates as part of being onboarded as an approved transport partner by a facility coordinator, with the vendor's consent obtained at that time. Message frequency varies with ride volume. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for assistance or STOP at any time to opt out of further messages.

4. We Do Not Sell or Share Mobile Numbers

We do not sell, rent, or share resident, vendor, or coordinator phone numbers with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Phone numbers are used solely to operate the Service — for example, to deliver ride dispatch SMS messages through our messaging provider (Twilio) — and are shared with service providers only as described in Section 6, strictly to the extent necessary to provide the Service.

5. Health-Related Information

Resident transport-needs information (such as mobility or medical equipment requirements) is collected only to the extent necessary to arrange safe and appropriate transportation, and is accessible only to authorized staff at the resident's facility and organization. Facilities using KinCare are responsible for ensuring their use of the Service complies with any applicable health information privacy obligations that apply to them, and, where such obligations apply, for entering into any additional data protection agreements required with KinCare.

6. Service Providers

We share information with the following categories of service providers, solely to operate the Service:

  • Twilio — SMS delivery to transport vendors.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — push notifications to coordinators.
  • LocationIQ — map display for live ride tracking.
  • Our hosting and database providers — to store and run the Service.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all traffic to and from the Service, password hashing, and role- and tenant-scoped access controls so that one organization's data is not visible to another. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as an organization maintains an active account with KinCare, and for a reasonable period afterward as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. An organization administrator may request deletion of their organization's data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion option in Settings.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make such a request, contact us using the information in Section 11.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of the Service after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to make a data request, contact us at privacy@kincare.app.
